Data Engineering
At AstroSpider, our Data Engineering services provide cutting-edge solutions to help you manage and analyze your data efficiently. We specialize in utilizing industry-leading tools such as Databricks for scalable data processing, Snowflake for cloud data warehousing, and a range of other platforms to build robust data pipelines and architectures. From ETL development to advanced data analytics, we ensure your data is optimized for real-time reporting and decision-making. Our services include:
  • Data pipeline design and optimization using Databricks, Spark, Airflow, and more.
  • Cloud data warehousing with Snowflake, AWS, GCP, and Azure
  • Database management and orchestration
  • Real-time analytics and data streaming
  • Advanced data modeling and architecture design
  • Integration with BI tools such as Power BI, Tableau, Qlik, and Spotfire
